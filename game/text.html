Text Adventure

You step into a dark cave where three paths diverge ahead.

Which path will you explore?

How to Play

Goal

Choose paths to progress a simple story. Each choice changes the next scene text.

Steps

  1. Read the story text shown on screen.
  2. Click Left / Middle / Right to choose a path.
  3. Buttons disable briefly; a new random scene appears.

Examples

  • Left: treasure, dragons, or getting lost (random).
  • Middle: trap escape or friendly elf help.
  • Right: meet a puppy or minor stumble.

Notes

  • Outcomes are random; replay to see alternatives.
  • Runs locally in your browser.
