Click the Block

Click as many blue blocks as you can in 20 seconds

How to Play

Goal

Click as many blue blocks as possible within 20 seconds. Each click scores +1.

Controls

  • Mouse or touch: click a blue block to score.
  • Blocks appear at random positions in a 400×400 area.

Scoring & Result

  • Timer counts down from 20 seconds.
  • When time is up, your total score is shown and a PNG snapshot is offered for download.

Examples

  • Casual try: score 10–20 by steady clicking.
  • Challenge: aim for 30+ by anticipating positions and clicking fast.

Tips

  • Use a larger screen or mouse for faster targeting.
  • Keep the cursor near the center to reduce travel time.

Notes

  • Game runs entirely in your browser; no data is uploaded.
  • Performance may vary with device speed and browser.
