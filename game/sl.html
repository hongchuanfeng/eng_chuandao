How to Play

Goal

Reveal all safe cells without clicking any mines. Use numeric clues to deduce mine positions.

Controls

  • Left click: reveal a cell.
  • Numbers indicate adjacent mine count; 0 auto-expands neighbors.

Win/Lose

  • Reveal all non‑mine cells to win.
  • Clicking a mine ends the game.

Timer & Score

  • Timer starts at reset and updates every second.
  • Score reflects revealed cells percentage.

Tips

  • Use patterns like 1‑2‑1, corners, and edges.
  • When uncertain, choose the least risky cell.

Notes

  • All logic runs locally in your browser.
  • Board: 15×15, Mines: 30.
