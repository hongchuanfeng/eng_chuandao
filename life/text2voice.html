Text to Speech

How to Use

This tool converts text to speech using your browser's built-in text-to-speech engine. You can choose between Chinese and English voices and download the audio.

Step-by-Step Instructions:

  1. Select Language: Choose between Chinese (China) or English (US)
  2. Enable Auto Play: Check "Auto play audio" if you want immediate playback
  3. Enter Text: Type or paste your text in the text area
  4. Play Audio: Click "Play" to start text-to-speech conversion
  5. Stop Audio: Click "Stop" to stop the current playback
  6. Download: Click "Download audio" to save the speech as an MP3 file

Language Options:

  • Chinese (China): Uses Chinese voice synthesis
  • English (US): Uses English voice synthesis

Features:

  • Real-time Playback: Listen to text as it's being converted
  • Auto Play: Automatically start playback when text is entered
  • Download Support: Save audio as MP3 files
  • Multiple Languages: Support for Chinese and English
  • Browser-based: No external services required
