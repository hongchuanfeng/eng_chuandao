Kinship Calculator

How to Use

This tool calculates the relationship between two family members based on their roles. It helps you understand how different family members are related to each other.

Step-by-Step Instructions:

  1. Select First Person: Choose the first family member from the dropdown menu
  2. Select Second Person: Choose the second family member from the dropdown menu
  3. Calculate: Click "Calculate Relationship" to see how they are related
  4. View Result: The relationship will be displayed in the result field

Available Family Members:

  • Parents: Father, Mother
  • Children: Son, Daughter
  • Grandparents: Grandfather, Grandmother
  • Grandchildren: Grandson, Granddaughter
  • Siblings: Brother, Sister

Examples:

  • Father + Son: "Father and son"
  • Mother + Daughter: "Mother and daughter"
  • Son + Son: "Brothers"
  • Daughter + Daughter: "Sisters"
  • Father + Grandson: "Grandfather"
  • Mother + Granddaughter: "Maternal grandmother"

Relationship Types:

  • Direct Relationships: Parent-child, grandparent-grandchild
  • Sibling Relationships: Brother-brother, sister-sister, brother-sister
  • Extended Family: Grandfather, grandmother, maternal/paternal relationships

Common Use Cases:

  • Understanding family tree relationships
  • Educational purposes for learning family structures
  • Genealogy research and family history
  • Cultural studies and anthropology
  • Family counseling and therapy

Important Notes:

  • This tool uses standard family relationship terminology
  • Results are based on traditional family structures
  • Some relationships may vary by cultural context
  • For complex family structures, consider consulting a genealogist
