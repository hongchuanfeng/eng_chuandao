Holiday Information

This table displays major traditional Chinese holidays with their lunar calendar dates and English names.

Featured Holidays

  • Spring Festival (春节): Chinese New Year - 1st day of Lunar January
  • Lantern Festival (元宵节): 15th day of Lunar January
  • Dragon Boat Festival (端午节): 5th day of Lunar May
  • Double Seventh (七夕节): 7th day of Lunar July (Chinese Valentine's Day)
  • Mid-Autumn Festival (中秋节): 15th day of Lunar August
  • Double Ninth Festival (重阳节): 9th day of Lunar September
  • Laba Festival (腊八节): 8th day of Lunar December
  • House Cleaning Day: 24th day of Lunar December

Lunar Calendar System

  • Chinese holidays follow the traditional lunar calendar
  • Dates vary each year in the Gregorian calendar
  • Lunar months are approximately 29-30 days long
  • Some years have 13 lunar months (leap year)
  • Holiday dates shift by about 11 days each year

Cultural Significance

  • Spring Festival: Most important holiday, family reunion time
  • Lantern Festival: End of New Year celebrations, lantern displays
  • Dragon Boat Festival: Commemorates poet Qu Yuan, dragon boat races
  • Double Seventh: Romantic holiday, similar to Valentine's Day
  • Mid-Autumn Festival: Moon viewing, family gatherings, mooncakes
  • Double Ninth: Respect for elderly, mountain climbing
  • Laba Festival: Buddhist holiday, eating Laba congee

Holiday Traditions

  • Spring Festival: Red decorations, firecrackers, family dinners
  • Lantern Festival: Lantern riddles, tangyuan (sweet dumplings)
  • Dragon Boat Festival: Zongzi (sticky rice dumplings), dragon boat races
  • Double Seventh: Romantic activities, gift exchanges
  • Mid-Autumn Festival: Mooncakes, family reunions, moon viewing
  • Double Ninth: Chrysanthemum viewing, mountain climbing
  • Laba Festival: Eating Laba congee, temple visits

Important Notes

  • Holiday dates change each year in the Gregorian calendar
  • Some holidays are official public holidays in China
  • Regional variations may exist in different parts of China
  • Modern celebrations may differ from traditional customs
  • Check current year calendars for exact dates

Business Considerations

  • Many businesses close during major holidays
  • Travel and transportation may be affected
  • Shipping and delivery services may be delayed
  • Banking and government services may be limited
  • Plan business activities accordingly
