How to Use

This tool implements the Pomodoro Technique, a time management method that uses timed intervals of work and breaks to improve productivity and focus.

Step-by-Step Instructions:

  1. Set Work Time: Enter your desired work session duration (default: 25 minutes)
  2. Set Break Time: Enter your desired break duration (default: 5 minutes)
  3. Start Work: Click "Start Work" to begin your first work session
  4. Work Session: Focus on your task until the timer reaches zero
  5. Break Time: Take a break when the work session ends
  6. Repeat: The cycle automatically repeats between work and break

Pomodoro Technique Basics:

  • Work Session: 25 minutes of focused work (adjustable)
  • Short Break: 5 minutes of rest (adjustable)
  • Long Break: 15-30 minutes after 4 work sessions
  • Cycle: Repeat work-break cycles throughout the day

Recommended Time Settings:

  • Beginners: 20-25 minutes work, 5 minutes break
  • Experienced: 25-30 minutes work, 5-10 minutes break
  • Long Tasks: 45 minutes work, 15 minutes break
  • Short Tasks: 15 minutes work, 3 minutes break

Features:

  • Customizable Timers: Set your own work and break durations
  • Fullscreen Mode: Automatically goes fullscreen during work sessions
  • Visual Timer: Large, easy-to-read countdown display
  • Automatic Cycling: Seamlessly switches between work and break
  • Alert System: Notifies you when sessions end

Common Use Cases:

  • Studying and academic work
  • Writing and creative projects
  • Programming and development
  • Household chores and tasks
  • Reading and research
  • Exercise and fitness routines

Tips for Better Results:

  • Choose a quiet, distraction-free environment
  • Turn off notifications during work sessions
  • Use break time for actual rest, not more work
  • Track your progress and adjust times as needed
  • Take longer breaks after 4 work sessions
  • Plan your tasks before starting the timer

Productivity Benefits:

  • Improved focus and concentration
  • Better time awareness and management
  • Reduced procrastination
  • Increased motivation and energy
  • Better work-life balance
  • Reduced mental fatigue

Important Notes:

  • This tool automatically goes fullscreen during work sessions
  • You can exit fullscreen by pressing Escape or closing the browser
  • Timers are based on 60-second intervals
  • Browser must remain open for timers to work
  • Consider using a physical timer for offline use
