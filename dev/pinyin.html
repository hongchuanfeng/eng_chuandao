About this tool:

  1. Generate Pinyin (with tone marks) from Chinese text.
  2. Choose Pinyin position and whether to include tone marks.
  3. Tip: Review generated Pinyin for correctness.

How to Use

What this tool does

Convert Chinese characters to Pinyin. Choose display position and optionally keep punctuation/letters or add spaces.

Steps

  1. Enter Chinese text into the textarea.
  2. Configure options (punctuation/letters/spaces, above/beside, tone marks).
  3. Click Convert to generate Pinyin.

Examples

中国人民很友好 → Zhōng guó rén mín hěn yǒu hǎo

Notes

  • Polyphonic characters may need manual adjustment.
  • Rendering uses tags when showing Pinyin above.
