About Unix time:

  1. Unix time (Epoch) starts at 1970-01-01 00:00 UTC.
  2. Widely used to represent seconds since the epoch.
  3. On 32-bit systems, timestamps overflow in 2038.
  4. 64-bit systems cover far beyond that range.

How to Use

What this tool does

Convert Unix timestamps (seconds) to human-readable Beijing time and convert date/time back to timestamps.

Steps

  1. Check the live current timestamp.
  2. Enter a timestamp and convert to Beijing time.
  3. Pick a date/time to convert back to a timestamp.
