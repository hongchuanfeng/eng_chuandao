How to Use the BMI Calculator

Calculate your Body Mass Index (BMI) quickly and easily.

Instructions

  • Enter your height in centimeters (cm)
  • Enter your weight in kilograms (kg)
  • Click the "Calculate" button
  • View your BMI result and body status

BMI Categories

  • Underweight: BMI less than 18.5
  • Normal: BMI between 18.5 and 23.9
  • Overweight: BMI between 24 and 27.9
  • Obese: BMI 28 or higher

Examples

Example 1: A person who is 175 cm tall and weighs 70 kg has a BMI of 22.9 (Normal).

Example 2: A person who is 160 cm tall and weighs 50 kg has a BMI of 19.5 (Normal).

Important Notes

BMI is a screening tool and does not directly measure body fat. It may not accurately reflect body composition for athletes, older adults, or people with high muscle mass. For personalized health advice, consult a healthcare professional.
