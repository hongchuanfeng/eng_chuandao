How to Use the Domain WHOIS Lookup

Check domain registration information, ownership details, and expiration dates for any domain name.

Instructions

  • Enter a domain name in the search box (e.g., example.com)
  • Click "Search" to retrieve WHOIS information
  • View registration details, ownership, and expiration dates
  • Check recent queries for quick access to previously searched domains

What Information is Displayed

  • Domain Name: The domain being queried
  • Registrar: Company that registered the domain
  • Created Date: When the domain was first registered
  • Expires Date: When the domain registration expires
  • Status: Current registration status
  • Country: Country of the registrant
  • State/Province: State or province of the registrant

Domain Status Types

  • Active: Domain is registered and active
  • Expired: Domain registration has expired
  • Pending Delete: Domain is scheduled for deletion
  • Redemption Period: Domain is in grace period after expiration

Examples

Example 1 - Active Domain:
Domain: google.com
Status: Active, Expires: 2025-09-14

Example 2 - Expired Domain:
Domain: expired-example.com
Status: Expired, Available for registration

Domain Expiration Process

  • Grace Period (30-45 days): Domain expires but can be renewed
  • Redemption Period (30 days): Domain requires special recovery process
  • Pending Delete (5 days): Domain will be released to public
  • Available: Domain can be registered by anyone

Use Cases

  • Check if a domain is available for registration
  • Research domain ownership and contact information
  • Monitor domain expiration dates
  • Investigate domain history and previous owners
  • Verify domain authenticity and legitimacy
  • Plan domain acquisition strategies

Privacy Considerations

  • Some registrars offer privacy protection services
  • Personal information may be hidden behind proxy services
  • Contact information shown may be for privacy service providers
  • Not all domains display complete ownership information

Tips for Domain Research

  • Check multiple variations of domain names
  • Look for typos and alternative spellings
  • Monitor domains that are close to expiration
  • Use the recent queries feature to track your searches
  • Consider different TLD extensions (.com, .net, .org, etc.)

Important Notes

  • WHOIS data may not always be complete or accurate
  • Some domains may have restricted or private registration
  • Information is provided for informational purposes only
  • Always verify information through official channels
  • Respect privacy and don't misuse contact information
