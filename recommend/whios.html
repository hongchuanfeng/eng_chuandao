Domain WHOIS Lookup

About WHOIS:

  • 1. Check if a domain is registered/expired and view registration details.
  • 2. Learn about handling domain expiration.
  • Generic domains:
    • (1) After expiry: 30–45 day grace period; limited changes allowed.
    • (2) Redemption period: ~30 days after grace period.
    • (3) Pending delete: ~5 days; then released to public.
  • Domestic domains (CN):
    • (1) 35-day grace renewal window.
    • (2) Then a 15-day redemption period; domain not accessible.
    • (3) After ~48 days, domain may be deleted at any time.
