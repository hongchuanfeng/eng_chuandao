Simplified/Traditional Chinese Converter

About Simplified and Traditional Chinese:

  • 1. Simplified Chinese is a writing system using simplified Chinese characters. It is officially used in Mainland China, Singapore, and Malaysia.
  • 2. Traditional Chinese is a writing system using traditional Chinese characters. It is mainly used in Taiwan, Hong Kong, Macau, and overseas Chinese communities.
  • 3. This tool converts between Traditional Chinese and Simplified Chinese in both directions.
