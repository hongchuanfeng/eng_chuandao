Simplified/Traditional Chinese Converter

About Simplified and Traditional Chinese:

  • 1. Simplified Chinese is a writing system using simplified Chinese characters. It is officially used in Mainland China, Singapore, and Malaysia.
  • 2. Traditional Chinese is a writing system using traditional Chinese characters. It is mainly used in Taiwan, Hong Kong, Macau, and overseas Chinese communities.
  • 3. This tool converts between Traditional Chinese and Simplified Chinese in both directions.

How to Use Simplified/Traditional Chinese Converter

Convert between Simplified and Traditional Chinese characters instantly.

Instructions

  • Enter Chinese text in the upper text area
  • Click "To Traditional Chinese" to convert to Traditional
  • Click "To Simplified Chinese" to convert to Simplified
  • Click "Clear" to remove all text
  • Results appear in the lower text area

Features

  • Bidirectional conversion: Convert both ways seamlessly
  • Instant results: Conversion happens immediately
  • Special character handling: Properly converts punctuation marks
  • Accurate mapping: Uses industry-standard conversion tables
