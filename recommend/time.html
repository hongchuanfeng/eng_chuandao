How to Use the World Time Table

Time Zone Information

This table displays the current time in major Asian cities with their respective time zones and UTC offsets.

Displayed Information

  • Country: The country where the city is located
  • City: Major city name in that time zone
  • Timezone: UTC offset and GMT notation
  • Date: Current date in YYYY-MM-DD format
  • Time: Current local time in HH:MM:SS format

Featured Time Zones

  • UTC+8 (GMT+8): China, Hong Kong, Malaysia, Singapore
  • UTC+9 (GMT+9): Japan, South Korea
  • UTC+7 (GMT+7): Vietnam, Thailand

Common Use Cases

  • International business meetings and conference calls

  • Event scheduling for global audiences
  • Stock market hours and trading times
  • Customer support across regions

Time Zone Conversion Tips

  • UTC+8 is 8 hours ahead of UTC (Coordinated Universal Time)
  • UTC+9 is 9 hours ahead of UTC
  • UTC+7 is 7 hours ahead of UTC
  • When it's noon in UTC+8, it's 1 PM in UTC+9 and 11 AM in UTC+7

Important Notes

  • Times are displayed in 24-hour format
  • All times shown are current local times
  • Some countries may observe daylight saving time (not shown here)
  • Times are updated automatically when the page loads
  • For real-time updates, refresh the page

Business Hours Reference

  • China/Hong Kong: Typically 9 AM - 6 PM local time
  • Japan/Korea: Typically 9 AM - 6 PM local time
  • Southeast Asia: Varies by country, generally 8 AM - 5 PM
