Table Overview

This reference table provides detailed information about major and minor Chinese dynasties, including their dates, capitals, and present-day locations.

What Information is Displayed?

  • Dynasty: Name of the dynasty or period
  • Years: Period of reign with precise dates
  • Capital: Capital city during the dynasty
  • Present location: Modern city/country location

Dynasty Categories

  • Major Dynasties: Main ruling periods (highlighted in blue)
  • Sub-dynasties: Subdivisions or regional powers (indented)
  • Unified Periods: Times when China was unified under one dynasty
  • Divided Periods: Times of fragmentation (e.g., Three Kingdoms)

Timeline Highlights

  • Ancient Period: Xia, Shang, Zhou (c. 2146 BCE - 256 BCE)
  • Imperial Era: Qin through Qing (221 BCE - 1911 CE)
  • Modern Era: Republic of China and People's Republic
